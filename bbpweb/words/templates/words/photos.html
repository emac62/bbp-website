Client Work

In addition to fine art photography, I am also a portrait photographer, specializing in headshots for performers and professionals as well as candid photos for engagements and weddings. If you are in need of professional photos, please drop me a note and we can work towards your new look. As we come out of the pandemic, I think we all look a little different!
