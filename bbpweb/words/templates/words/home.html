Words

This website will carry regular blogs about events, the process of taking photos, photo subjects and other cathartic moments that may arise. The purpose of this blog is to provide time and space to the idea of artistic investigation. It is not meant to explain what I have done, but rather sit on its own and to help develop greater perspective or story about my work. I hope that you find some interest in this exploration. By joining the mailing list, you will receive notice of new piece of writing and/or photo project. We will not send more than one notice per entry.
