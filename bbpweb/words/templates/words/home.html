bobbieb

Starting a website is a natural progression from being too busy and then finally having the time to organize and shape pictures that I have taken. As a career artist educator, my pursuits have (thankfully) been entwined with my students and I am so much richer for it. Now though, I am able to finally focus on my style and selfishly work on personal projects. Making suggestions to others for so many years has made me acutely aware of the roles that the audience and artist have - and how they need one another. I am excited and also afraid of the next phase in my artistic journey.

Having my private artistic expression of photography and words revealed to the world is rather like having the door to the outhouse fly open and not being able to grab the handle in time. But, sharing is the obligation of each artist - as a piece is opened to the world learning about art and ourselves becomes deeper. Join me in the pursuit of honing fine photos and words that investigate particular moments.

You will also find that I am open to portrait work and some wedding photography. Send me a note and we can collaborate.
